CirTrack Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

1. Overview

CirTrack is a diet management and fitness tracking application available on iOS and macOS. We follow the principle of “data minimization.” By default, user data is stored locally on the device. Services such as iCloud, HealthKit, and StoreKit are used only with your explicit authorization to enable synchronization or other related features.

1.1 Purchase Verification Information (StoreKit)

To verify subscription or in-app purchase status, we receive necessary purchase verification data from Apple, including:

  • Purchase status (whether you have active subscription benefits)
  • Transaction identifiers and required verification fields

We do not have access to your payment method or billing details.

1.2 iCloud Synchronization (Optional)

If you enable iCloud, the following data may be synchronized:

  • Diet records (timestamps, photos, text)
  • Workout and training records
  • To-do items, check-ins, countdowns, timers, storage records, and related data

1.3 HealthKit (Optional)

With your authorization, CirTrack may read the following health data:

  • Body weight, steps, heart rate, sleep, active energy, distance

1.4 App Analytics (Anonymous, Optional)

If you enable “Share Analytics with Developers” at the system level, Apple may provide us with anonymous crash reports and performance data.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Payment card numbers or billing addresses
  • Any personal content without your authorization
  • We do not sell or share your data

3. Purpose of Data Usage

  • To verify subscription or membership status
  • To provide core application functionality
  • To improve stability using anonymous data

4. Third-Party Services Used

Service Purpose
StoreKit In-app purchase verification
iCloud / CloudKit Data synchronization
HealthKit Reading health data
Photo Library Access Photo selection and storage (diet records, storage items, etc.)

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Local data is stored on your device and can be deleted at any time. iCloud data is managed by Apple and can be removed through your iCloud settings.

6. Data Security

  • We do not operate our own servers (data remains on device or iCloud)
  • iCloud data is encrypted and protected by Apple
  • Crash logs are anonymous

7. Children’s Privacy

This application is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect such information.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest update date will be displayed at the top of this page.
