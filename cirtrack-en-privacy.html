CirTrack Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-12-05

1. Overview

CirTrack is an iOS and macOS application for diet tracking and fitness management. We follow a strict data-minimization approach: data stays on your device by default and is synced only with your permission through Apple services such as iCloud, HealthKit and StoreKit.

1.1 Purchase Verification (StoreKit)

To validate in-app purchases, we receive necessary entitlement information from Apple, such as:

  • Your purchase status
  • Transaction identifiers used for validation

No payment card or billing info is accessible to us.

1.2 iCloud Sync (optional)

If you enable iCloud, synced data may include:

  • Diet logs (time, photos, notes)
  • Food library, custom foods and tags
  • Workout plans and logs
  • Tasks, check-ins, countdowns
  • App settings and multi-user data

1.3 HealthKit (optional)

With your permission, CirTrack may read:

  • Weight, steps, heart rate, sleep, active energy, distance

1.4 App Analytics (anonymous)

If enabled in system settings, Apple may share anonymized crash and performance reports to help improve the app.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No credit card or billing data
  • No personal content without your explicit consent
  • Your data is never sold or shared with third parties

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Validate your purchase entitlement
  • Enable app features such as syncing and logging
  • Improve performance (anonymous diagnostics only)

5. Data Retention

Local data stays on your device and can be deleted anytime. iCloud data is stored and managed by Apple.

6. Data Security

  • No developer-operated servers (data stays on device or iCloud)
  • iCloud storage is encrypted
  • Crash logs are anonymized

7. Children's Privacy

CirTrack does not target children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their information.
