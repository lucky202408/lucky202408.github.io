AppAssets Privacy Policy Privacy First

Last Updated: January 8, 2026

1. Overview

AppAssets respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how AppAssets handles data when you use the application. AppAssets is designed as a local-first tool for creating and managing App Store screenshots, app icons, and related assets.

2. Data Collection

AppAssets does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

  • No account registration required
  • No analytics tracking
  • No third-party advertising SDKs
  • No personal data collection

3. Local Data Processing

All images, screenshots, icons, and related assets are processed entirely on your device.

  • Files remain on your device
  • No cloud upload by default
  • No remote processing

4. Membership & Export Feature

AppAssets provides both free and paid features. The export functionality is available only to Lifetime Members.

  • Free users can create and preview assets
  • Exporting files is restricted to paid members
  • Purchase and verification are handled via Apple StoreKit

Membership status is verified locally on your device. AppAssets does not access or store your payment information.

5. In-App Purchases

AppAssets offers a one-time Lifetime Membership purchase. All payments are processed securely by Apple.

  • No subscriptions
  • No recurring charges
  • Purchases can be restored via Apple ID

6. Third-Party Services

AppAssets does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

7. Children’s Privacy

AppAssets does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
